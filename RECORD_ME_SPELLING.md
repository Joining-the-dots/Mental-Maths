# RECORD ME — Spellcraft words + sentences (359 entries)

For **each entry**: say the number as a marker, then the WORD, a clear one-second pause, the SENTENCE, another clear pause, then the WORD once more:

> "Number one. …the. (pause) The dog wagged its happy tail. (pause) …the."

The number is only a splitting marker — it gets cut out. Each take becomes two game clips: the word alone (Hear the word) and word-sentence-word (In a sentence). The pauses are what let the split happen, so make them deliberate. Fluffed one? Say the same number again and redo it — the last take of each number wins. Record in one go or in chunks; drop the file(s) into OneDrive → Times tables → recordings.
